I am a numerical cosmologist working as a postdoc on large cosmological hydrodynamic simulations at the Observatory of Trieste (Italy). My main interests are formation and evolution of dark matter haloes and High Performance Computing (HPC) code re-engineering in order to run high-resolution gravity+hydrodynamic solvers on GPU and accelerators.
