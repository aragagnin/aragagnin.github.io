I am an astrophysicist and I work as post-doc at the University of Bologna. My main interests are: numerical simulations of formation and evolution of galaxy clusters, thorugh the study of their evolution, concentration, X-ray thermal emissions, optical properties, and strong lensing effects. I am also interested in High Performance Computing (HPC), as re-engineering of gravity and hydrodynamic N-body solvers in order to run the next generation of high-resolution hydrodynamic simulations on cosmological volumes.
