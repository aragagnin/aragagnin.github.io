Cosmology-dependent masses and concentrations from hydrodynamic Magneticum simulations

This webpage allows to use the cosmology-dependent mass-concentration (Mc) and mass-mass (MM) from Magneticum hydrodynamic simulations conversion relations presented in Ragagnin et al. (2020). Here below you can insert cosmological parameters ( Ωm, Ωb, σ8, h0 ), masses, scale factors (or redshifts) and overdensities Δ=vir, 200c, 500c, 2500c, 200m. The conversion routines used in this web-app are also available as a python package github.com/aragagnin/hydro_mc. In the Output field you can choose between the cΔ (default) or any MΔ2 values.

We used Brython to open the access of our python library to this web-app. For question or additional information pabout this page please contact Antonio Ragagnin (antonio.ragagnin at inaf.it).
